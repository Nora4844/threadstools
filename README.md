# ThreadsTools: free tools for Threads

[ThreadsTools](https://threadstools.app) is a small, free toolbox for people who post on Threads. No login, no watermark, nothing to install.

## What it does

- **Download** the video, photos, GIF, or audio from any public Threads post
- **[Threads to MP3](https://threadstools.app/tools/threads-audio-downloader)** pulls just the audio, right in your browser
- **Screenshot** a post into a clean image, or make a quote card or mockup
- **Convert** an X tweet, a LinkedIn post, or a blog into a ready Threads draft

Everything runs in your browser, free, with no sign-up. The full set is at https://threadstools.app/tools
