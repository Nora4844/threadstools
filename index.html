ThreadsTools

ThreadsTools is a small, free toolbox for people who post on Threads. No login, no watermark, nothing to install.

What it does

  • Download the video, photos, GIF, or audio from any public Threads post
  • Threads to MP3: pull just the audio, right in your browser
  • Screenshot a post into a clean image, or make a quote card or mockup
  • Convert an X tweet, a LinkedIn post, or a blog into a ready Threads draft

Everything runs in your browser, free, with no sign-up. The full set is at threadstools.app/tools.
